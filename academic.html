About the Academics Hub

The Academics Hub is a central resource on the Smart Curriculum Uganda platform, designed to provide students, teachers, and schools with curated academic content aligned with the new Ugandan curriculum. It consolidates key academic tools for effective learning and curriculum implementation.

Subject Resources

Access syllabuses, notes, and lesson guides tailored to the Ugandan curriculum.

Revision Materials

Download revision booklets, past papers, and marking guides for all levels.

Learning Aids

Explore videos, animations, simulations, and interactive tools for better learning.

Competency Tasks

Find practical projects and assessment tasks aligned with curriculum competencies.

Impact

Through the Academics Hub, thousands of learners across Uganda gain access to digital syllabuses, revision tools, and interactive content. It bridges the resource gap between urban and rural schools, driving national academic transformation.
