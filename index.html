About Smart Curriculum Uganda

Smart Curriculum Uganda is an innovative digital platform dedicated to transforming the delivery and accessibility of the new Ugandan curriculum. Built with a STEM foundation, it integrates academics, performance tracking, career tools, and community collaboration in one space.

Our mission is to empower students, teachers, schools, and communities by making curriculum resources engaging, accessible, and useful through smart technology and collaborative design.

Project Developers: This platform was crafted by a passionate team of young innovators led by Jjuuko James Jordan and the Vajoresko initiative, in partnership with local educators and learners.

We believe that through STEM, creativity, and local collaboration, Uganda can shape a smart, self-driven academic future.
